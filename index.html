🍜 Food

Toronto is an excellent vegan city. These are all either fully vegan or very vegan-friendly.

Sit-down / dinner

Hawker
All-vegan Southeast Asian small plates in Kensington Market. Cool patio, art-y interior. Good pre-show dinner.

⚡ Practical Notes

  • 👟 Walking festival — all venues are in the Annex or nearby. Comfortable shoes matter more than anything else you pack.
  • 📱 Download the DICE app before you go — your tickets live there. Don't assume you can pull them up in a browser at the door.
  • 🔞 Most venues are 19+. Trinity St. Paul's and Tranzac are all-ages. Bring ID regardless.
  • 🗺 Lee's Palace and Trinity St. Paul's are both on Bloor St W, about a 3-minute walk apart. Tranzac is one block south on Brunswick. Plan your venue hops accordingly.
  • 🍺 Tranzac has its own bar and a more relaxed vibe — good spot to decompress between heavy sets.
  • 📅 Trail of Dead (performing Source Tags & Codes + Secret of Elena's Tomb in full) is listed as Friday but didn't appear in the timetable image at time of writing — double check the schedule on the PTG site before Friday.
