🍜 Food

Toronto is an excellent vegan city. These are all either fully vegan or very vegan-friendly. The Annex and Kensington are particularly well-covered.

Breakfast & coffee

Almond Butterfly Bakeshop
Gluten-free café + bakery on Harbord. Daily 9am–7pm. Best morning stop — dairy-free options, pastries, coffee. One block south of Bloor.
📍 100 Harbord St · maps
Fresh Kitchen + Juice Bar
Fully vegan. Daily 11am–10pm (10:30pm weekends). On Bloor — great for lunch or a weekend brunch between days. Cauliflower wings, poutine, bowls.
📍 386 Bloor St W · maps

Sit-down / dinner

Hawker
All-vegan Southeast Asian small plates in Kensington Market. Cool patio, art-y interior. Reserve ahead — good first-night dinner. Wed–Sun.
📍 291 Augusta Ave, Kensington · maps
The Hogtown Vegan
Southern comfort food — vegan wings, mac, poutine. Heavy and satisfying pre-show fuel. Wed–Sun from 4pm (weekends from 2pm).

PLANTA
Upscale all-vegan: creative sushi, dim sum, pasta. Two locations (Yorkville & Queen West). Treat-yourself tier — reserve ahead.

Selam Ethiopian
Strong vegan platter, sometimes live music. Daily from 4pm (weekends from noon). On Bloor West — good slower-pace dinner option.

🎵 Record Stores

Five stores within reach, all worth a browse. Emissions is the one for this trip — punk/HC vinyl, 80s show flyers on the walls. The Spadina cluster (Sonic Boom, Invisible City, Play De Record) are all within a block of each other.

Emissions Record Shop ★
Best punk/HC vinyl in Toronto. 80s show flyers on the walls, basement location. Tue–Sun (closed Mon). This is the priority — go Thursday or Friday afternoon.
📍 1114 Dundas St W (basement) · maps
Sonic Boom
Two floors, all genres. Daily 11am–8pm on Spadina. The reliable browse — if you're heading to Kensington, it's right there.
📍 215 Spadina Ave · maps
Invisible City
Curated and rare. Two turntables to listen before buying. Opens 1pm daily (Thu–Sat until 8pm). Same block as Sonic Boom — go after.
📍 222 Spadina Ave, Unit C4 · maps
Play De Record
Electronic and hip-hop institution. Mon + Wed–Sat (closed Tue/Sun). Also on Spadina — easy to hit as part of the same trip.
📍 411 Spadina Ave · maps
Dead Dog Records
Further west on Bloor. Good selection, worth the walk if you're already heading that direction. Hours vary.
📍 1277 Bloor St W · maps

⚡ Practical Notes

  • 👟 Walking festival — all venues are in the Annex or nearby. Comfortable shoes matter more than anything else you pack.
  • 📱 Download the DICE app before you go — your tickets live there. Screenshot your QR code before flying. Don't assume you can pull them up in a browser at the door.
  • 🔞 Most venues are 19+. Trinity St. Paul's and Tranzac are all-ages. Bring ID regardless.
  • 🗺 Lee's Palace and Trinity St. Paul's are both on Bloor St W, about a 3-minute walk apart. Tranzac is one block south on Brunswick. Dance Cave is upstairs in the same building as Lee's Palace — two flights up.
  • ⚠️ Popular shows are not guaranteed entry — arrive one set early to hold your spot. Pallbearer, Oathbreaker, Svalbard, and Amenra's acoustic set will fill fast.
  • 🍺 Tranzac has its own bar and a more relaxed vibe — good spot to decompress between heavy sets.
  • 🌙 Guerrilla Burger / Happy Taco (454 Bloor W) is open every night until midnight — fully vegan, right on Bloor. This is the after-show failsafe every night of the festival.
  • 🇯🇲 Veggie D'Light (160 Baldwin St, Kensington) is Jamaican vegan and only open Fri–Sun 12–6pm. Don't miss the window — it's a trip highlight. Arrive before 2pm to beat a wait.
  • ✈️ Take the UP Express from Pearson (YYZ) to Union or Bloor–Yonge Station — ~25 min, $12.35 CAD. Much better than a cab. Get a Presto card at the airport or any subway station for TTC transit.
  • 💰 CAD is roughly 1.35 per USD — budget accordingly. Confirm your card has no foreign transaction fees before flying.
  • 📖 Contact Editions (491 Davenport Rd) closes at 4pm Saturday — go before festival doors open if you want to browse rare/antiquarian books.
  • 🎞 Wrekmeister Harmonies are performing their original score live to their film Flowers in the Spring. Sunday night, The Cave — plan accordingly.
  • ❌ Trail of Dead (previously announced) have been removed from the lineup entirely. They do not appear on the official timetable or full lineup page.
